Once upon a time, I joined the AS/400 Performance Support team at IBM. My manager coerced me into sitting next to Zilla, so I could "learn more".

I knew it would be entertaining when, within the first hour, Zilla stared intently at his monitor while vacuously repeating "fuzzy bunies fuzzy bunnies" over and over to himself.

My goal with the website is to share the sitting next to Zilla experience, make you laugh, and provide a few minutes of enjoyable diversion from your work day.
